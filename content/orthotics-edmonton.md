### H2: What Are Custom Orthotics?
Custom orthotics are medical devices prescribed by healthcare professionals to support and align your feet and lower limbs. Unlike generic shoe inserts found in pharmacies, custom orthotics are:

- **Made for YOUR feet** using precise measurements and 3D scanning
- **Designed for YOUR specific condition** based on clinical assessment

### H2: Conditions Treated with Custom Orthotics in Edmonton

#### Plantar Fasciitis
The most common cause of heel pain. Custom orthotics provide arch support and cushioning to reduce strain on the plantar fascia.

#### Flat Feet (Pes Planus)
Lack of natural arch can cause overpronation, leading to knee, hip, and back pain. Orthotics restore proper alignment.

#### High Arches (Pes Cavus)
Excessive arch height creates pressure points and instability. Custom orthotics distribute weight evenly.

#### Bunions and Hallux Valgus
Orthotics can slow progression and relieve pressure on the big toe joint.

#### Metatarsalgia
Pain in the ball of the foot responds well to orthotics with metatarsal pads and cushioning.

#### Achilles Tendonitis
Proper foot alignment reduces strain on the Achilles tendon during activity.

#### Shin Splints
Often caused by overpronation; orthotics correct biomechanics to prevent recurrence.
